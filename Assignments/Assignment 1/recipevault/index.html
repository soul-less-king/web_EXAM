Quick Cooking Tips

  1. Read the full recipe before you start cooking
  2. Prep all ingredients (mise en place) before turning on the stove
  3. Season as you go -- taste frequently
  4. Let meat rest after cooking for juicier results
  5. Use a sharp knife -- it is safer and more efficient
